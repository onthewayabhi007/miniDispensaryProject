Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the dispensary's operating hours?

The Timings are:
Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM
Sunday: 9:00AM to 1:00PM

2. Do I need an appointment to visit the dispensary?

No,You need not to get an appointment, you may visit the dispensary directly

3. What services are provided at the dispensary?

All the services like First-Aid,Blood Pressure Measurement, Weight Measurement, Consultancy of the Allopathic,Ayurvedic, Homeopathic,Physiotherapist etc. are available here

4. What are the eligibility criteria to use the dispensary's services?

To use the dispensary ammenities and equipments you need to be a student or a teaching staff or non-teaching staff or the family member of them.

5. What documents do I need to bring with me for my appointment?

You must carry your college identity card, if you are a family member you should carry your ID along with the ID of the concerned head of the family

6. Are there any fees for using the dispensary's services?

No, the dispensary services including the medicines are completely free of cost

7. Does the dispensary offer any ambulance service?

Yes, the dispensary have its own ambulance service. You may call at +91 9549650438 to contact the ambulance

8. Is there a pharmacy on-site, or is there a pharmacy nearby?

The dispensary have onsite pharmacy which provide all the medicines free of cost

9. Can I see a specialist at the dispensary, or will I need a referral?

Specialists visit the dispensary at certain day of the week. The details of them is mentioned on the site itself in Doctor's section.

10. Are there any restrictions on the number of appointments I can make?

No, You may make appointments and visit doctors as many as you want.
